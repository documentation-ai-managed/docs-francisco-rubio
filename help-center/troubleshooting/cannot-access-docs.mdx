---
title: Cannot Access Documentation Space
description: Fix access problems in Francisco Rubio's documentation.
---

## Symptoms

- Login page reloads or gives an error
- Documentation does not load after login

## Possible Causes

- Incorrect email or password
- No internet connection
- Browser cache issue

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Double-check your email and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Clear browser cache and try again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test connection">
    Make sure you are online and able to reach `https://docs.franciscorubio.com`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
