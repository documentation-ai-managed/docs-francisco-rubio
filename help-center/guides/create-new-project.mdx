---
title: Create a New Project
description: Quick guide to starting a new documentation project.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to `https://docs.franciscorubio.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start new project">
    Click the "Create Project" button on the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill details">
    Enter the project name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up sections">
    Add sections for your docs, such as "Getting Started" or "API".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and share">
    Click "Save". Use the share link to invite your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
